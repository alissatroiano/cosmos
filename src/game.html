Cosmos

Cosmos is a physics-based space game. The main objective of Cosmos is to move around your orbit as many times as possible! Each time you complete three orbits without colliding with an enemy planet, you score a point and move on to the next level. You lose Cosmos if and when you collide with another planet.

When the game ends, press 'R' on your keyboard or click the reload icon to play again!

How to Play

The planet you control will always be the purple planet on the LEFT track

If you're playing on Desktop, use the Arrow Up and Arrow Down keys on your keyboard to control your planet's movement.

If you're playing on Mobile, Click the arrow controls on the bottom left of the page to control your planet's speed. .
